Simple LogBook App

Simple LogBook App V1.0

Simple LogBook ia a simple an easy to use, but effective IOS application. It designed for pilots all over the world who want to calculate own total flight hours.

I created this app for me and my friends but now I want to share it for all pilots. Now there are different programs for calculating your flight time, but they are so complicated... If you are: - Privat Pilot - General Aviation Pilot - Experimental Aviation Pilot - Student you can use this app Usage of it doesn't require you to any special skills. They application is designed in a such way that you only need to press one main button and record your flight. You will not get confused in the application menu. Only ONE MAIN BUTTON! And of course you can share and backup you log by many different ways, even iMessage ;)
